# King of the Hill Mode

## Zone
- A marked area on the terrain (glowing ring/beam)
- Player must stand inside to score points

## Scoring
- Points accumulate while the player is inside the zone
- Score target to win (e.g. first to 100) or endless survival variant

## Zombie Behavior
- Zombies are attracted to the hill zone
- Creates waves of enemies as the player holds position

## UI
- Score progress bar, current points, zone status indicator

## Shields Synergy
- Standing in the zone reduces regen delay by 0.5s
- Every 10 Hill points earned restores 25 shields
